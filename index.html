COEP Technological University

COEP Technological University (COEP Tech), chartered in 1854 is a nationally respected leader in technical education. The institute is distinguished by its commitment to finding solutions to the great predicaments of the day through advanced technology. The institute has a rich history and dedication to the pursuit of excellence. COEP Tech offers a unique learning experience across a spectrum of academic and social experiences.

With a firm footing in truth and humanity, the institute gives you an understanding of both technical developments and the ethics that go with it. The curriculum is designed to enhance your academic experience through opportunities like internships, study abroad programmes and research facilities. The hallmark of COEP education is its strong and widespread alumni network, support of the industry and the camaraderie that the institute shares with several foreign universities. MindSpark

MindSpark is the annual, national-level technical festival of COEP Tech. Since its inception in 2007, MindSpark has been igniting innovation and creativity. Evolving from modest beginnings, it celebrates intellect, teamwork, and competition. We aspire to give thinkers a stage to speak and to give creators an opportunity to build by transpiring revolution though innovation.

Over the years, MindSpark has embraced both offline and hybrid editions, reaching a wider audience and empowering young minds nationwide. With a vision of transforming lives through technology, we offer workshops, competitions, and immersive experiences, inspiring the next generation of thinkers and creators. Join us at MindSpark and be a spark of change, unlocking the boundless potential of the human mind among many like you, we are a community that strives towards excellence through innovation.
